BIOLOGICAL WALL FIELD

Distributed Wall Field Explorer

The assembly geometry becomes a living pruning interface: top zone, guidance cells, root-cap stress, Venturi drying and distributed root architecture in one interactive module.

Boundary programming -> volume architecture

We do not control roots directly. The wall creates a geometric environment: airflow patterns, moisture gradients, temperature differentials and guidance zones for root growth. The root system responds to this distributed field, not to a single cone or opening.

Air pruning does not stop growth; it redirects growth. Programming occurs at the boundary, while growth reorganizes inside the substrate volume.
